SeasonShifters
This game is about Frank. Frank is just a normal guy that lives a normal live in a normal town called Summerville.
People from Summerville are always relaxed, even now, when a big crisis is facing them.
Evildoers stole the "Rod of Ages" and destroyed the "Crystal of Seasons". The latter is responsible for the balance between all four Seasons in the whole kingdom of Lumia.
Will you help Frank find the vanished "Rod of Ages" and become the hero Lumia needs?
Solve countless puzzles and find all the pieces of the "Crystal of Seasons" to save the kingdom Lumia
before it dissapears between the seasons.
